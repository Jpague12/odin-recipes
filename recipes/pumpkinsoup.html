Pumpkin Soup!

This creamy and delicious pumpkin soup is all you need for this summer's festivies

It's hot, and will make you even hotter on a nice 100 degree day!

Ingredients

  • One pretty big pumpkin grown outside on your deck
  • Fifteen pounds of garlic
  • One pound of onions
  • 20 pounds of ghost pepper

Directions

  1. First, smash up that pumpkin with a large axe
  2. Next add that ghost pepper in. DO NOT forget to do this. We want this soup to be as hot as possible
  3. And lastly enjoy! You can really ground this stuff up in your tummy!
