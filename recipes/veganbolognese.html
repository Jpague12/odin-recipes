Vegan Bolognesee

This is a fantastic recipe you can make for the entire family!

Overall, it's super easy to make and provides so much protein you will go crazy with how much protein there is!

  • 5 pounds of big green basil
  • 20 pounds of garlic
  • some parmesan cheese even though this is a vegan recipe

  2. Next add the spaghetti in there and see what happens. You might wana add some water
  3. Finally, serve it with a bunch of cheese even though this recipe is vegan
  4. Serve it with cheese, trust me
