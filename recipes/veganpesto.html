Vegan Pesto

Normally with vegan pesto you may want to include basil, garlic, and some pine nuts.

But this recipe actually you can use whatever green leaf you'd like. You can use kale, spinach, whatever you want!

Ingredients

  • kale
  • pine nuts
  • garlic
  • olive oil

Directions

  1. First, wash kale gently massaging it under cold water
  2. Lightly toast the pine nuts until golden brown
  3. Place all 4 ingredients in food processor and pulse until desired consistency
